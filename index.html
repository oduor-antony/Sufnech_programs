SUFNECH represents "Support for Needy Children," focusing on empowering underprivileged children, particularly orphans and vulnerable Children (OVCs) in Migori County, Kenya. Migori County faces significant poverty challenges, particularly affecting children, women, and the elderly. SUFNECH is committed to providing holistic care and support to underprivileged children in the region.
Our mission is to assist disadvantaged children in Migori county, especially orphans and vulnerable children to overcome poverty.
We envision a vibrant community where each child has the opportunity to lead a healthy, educated, and prosperous life.

What We Do?

Healthy Food

SUFNECH Charity Organization provides nutritious food to the poor and needy in Migori County through bi-monthly food package distributions, targeting children facing hunger and supporting their host families effectively.

Pure Water

We collaborates with local communities to provide clean water access by installing boreholes and water tanks, ensuring the poor and needy in Migori County have safe drinking water.

Health Care

SUFNECH supports healthcare for the poor and needy in Migori County by enrolling beneficiaries in insurance plans and partnering with local clinics to provide essential medical services

Primary Education

We plans to establish primary schools in Migori County to provide quality education for the poor and needy, ensuring access to learning materials, uniforms, and a supportive environment.

Social Care

We offers social care to the poor and needy in Migori County by providing counseling, community support programs, and advocacy services to enhance well-being and foster a nurturing environment.
